Atal Tinkering Laboratories

ATL is a key program under Atal Innovation Mission, aiming to foster a culture of innovation among school students. The idea is to give wings to student's curiosity, creativity and imagination. In the first round itself, over 450 schools across India have already been provided with financial aid to make this vision a reality, while applications for second round are being invited and processed.

ATLs are intended to be a workspace within school, where students can shape thier ideas into reality using the tools and equipment that drive this modern era of technology.

Where do we come in

While government is encouraging the spread of STEM education through ATL, our expertise in the domain will facilitate the scheme's successful implementation. We welcome all the selected schools to exploit our expertise as we are now providing free consultation in the form of-

  • Guidance in understanding component specifications.
  • Online support in getting started with a component.

Apart from providing the consultation and support to help schools in taking knowledgeable decisions while procuring components and equipment for ATL, we have also designed a DIY based curriculum to kickstart your labwork, based on our DIY kit - Maker's Blackbox.
And not only that, we can also provide standardized components from open-source domain for overall setup of ATL in your institution. To get the things up and running at minimum cost, we also provide DIY based curriculum, for the whole, along with a Teacher's Training programme at your institution itself. With this, your institution will be able to manage the ATL in true sense. On the other hand, the option of having a professional mentor available in your premises to take care of the ATL and its functionality is always there.
